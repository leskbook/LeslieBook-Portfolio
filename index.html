About Me

I am a Cisco certified Lead Network Engineer with over 30 years of experience in the technology field. I started my career as a user support specialist and worked my way up to a server administrator back in the day when Novell was popular. I transitioned into network engineering and found my passion. I have worked in very small environments but currently work in a very large environment. Network engineering allows me to get my hands on the latest and greatest technology. Besides trying my best to learn full stack I am currently working towards my AWS certifications. I am also a career learner, achieving my masters in philosophy and religeous studies. My intention is to achieve my PhD in philosophy in the near future.
